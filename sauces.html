    • Sauces

    Enhance your hotpot experience with a variety of dipping sauces. From spicy chili sauces to savory soy-based mixes, the right sauce adds an extra layer of flavor to your hotpot ingredients. Feel free to experiment and find your perfect sauce combination. Here are some popular choices to get you started.

    Sesame Sauce

    Sesame sauce is suitable for tamato soup base, pork bone soup base, seafood soup base and mushroom soupbase. This sauce was originated from Northen-east China, made from grinded sesame paste.

    - Sesame paste 3 spoons

    - Cooked sesame 1 spoon

    - Chopped tabasco pepper A little

    Spicy and Sour sauce

    Spicy and sour dipping sauces is specially suitable for pork bone broth. It contains various of ingredients to enrich the level of the flavourable taste.

    - Soy sauce 1 spoon

    - Vinegar 1 spoon

    - Mashed garlic A little

    - Chopped tabasco pepper A little

    - Chili oil A little

    - Sesame oil A little

    Spicy dipping sauce

    Spicy dipping sauece is suitable for Spicy beef tallow soup and Vegetable-made chili oil soup. This dipping sauce will further enhances the taste of the spicyness.

    - Chili oil 3 spoons

    - Original soup of the Soup base 1 spoon

    - Crisp Soybean A little

    - Chopped celery A little

    - Corriander A little

    Seafood dipping sauce

    The Seafood dipping sauce is suitable for tomato soup base, pork bone soup base, seafood soup base and mushroom soup base. It is more tasty when it is matched with seafood.

    - XO sauce 1 spoon

    - Sacha sauce 1 spoon

    - Soy sauce 1 spoon

    - Sesame seasoning 1/2 spoon

    - Chili oil Appropriate amount
