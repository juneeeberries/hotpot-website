    • Cooking

    After you have Soup base selected, raw food prepared and portioned in plates, sauces prepared based on personal preferences, it's time to cook! Put the food into the simmering pot for 15 seconds to 1 minute (depends on the food that's cooked). When it's done, enjoy with dipping sauces. Refill broth and add ingredients as needed. Finish with noodles or rice. Hotpot is a social dining experience where everyone cooks and enjoys together, sometimes it can be lasts hours.
